MINIGOLF — Hole 3: The Dogleg & Windmill

Same constructible geometry in all four: rectangular course, wooden 2×6 perimeter walls, one interior wall creating an L-shape dogleg, rectangular pond and sand trap, windmill in the second chamber. Every wall is straight and at right angles — you could lay it out with real lumber. Only the aesthetic treatment changes.

1 · Ravensburger Illustrated

Painted textures, warm boardgame-page look. Playful, timeless, hand-crafted.

2 · Photo-Drone Realism

Overhead of a real Putt-Putt: fine astroturf, wood-grain planks, water reflections.

3 · Nintendo Switch Cheerful

Bright, saturated, clean rectangles with soft shadows. Phone/console friendly.

4 · Blueprint / Builder's Plan

Muted architectural feel with dimension marks. Looks like a real construction spec.
